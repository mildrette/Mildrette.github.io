About Us

What to know about Afkanerd

At Afkanerd, we're driven by a simple yet powerful idea: "We code for the people." Our mission is rooted in our firm belief in fundamental human rights, including freedom of speech and expression.

What we do

We leverage cutting-edge technologies to develop open-source software solutions that directly benefit individuals and communities. Our focus areas include healthcare, education, and communication.

WHY WE DO IT

Inspired by the actions of visionaries before us, we lead by example to inspire change and progress. Our goal is to embody the change we wish to see in the world.

HOW TO JOIN US

Everyone is welcome to contribute to our open-source projects, regardless of experience level. Together, we can innovate, grow, and shape a brighter future. Join us in advocating for the open-source culture. Explore our projects and get involved today!
